# Remove Algae from Reef

## Subsystems

1. Intake
1. Drivetrain
1. Camera
1. Elevator

## Assumptions 

* There are algae in the reef
* The obstacles in our path are static 
* We have the mechanism to remove algae

## Operations 

`removeAlgae`
* The Robot locates the algae in the reef
* Robot drives to the Reef
* The elevator needs to adjust to the correct height to remove the algae from the reef. 
* Intake the Algae
