# Process Algae 

## Subsystems

1. Algae Roller
1. Drivetrain
1. Camera
1. Elevator


## Assumptions

* We already have an algae in our robot 
* There are no obstacles in our path to processor

## Operations 

`processAlgae`
* The robot locates processor by estimating position
* The robot drives to the processor
* The elevator and the Algae Roller adjust to the correct position of the processor
* Eject Algae into processor
